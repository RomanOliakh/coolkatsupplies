FAQs

What is the Bouquet Builder?

The Bouquet Builder is a Build-Your-Own balloon bouquet tool! Builder has 1000+ balloons
for you to customize so you can pick out just the right colour and topper for your next
celebration. It's brand new and causing quite the stir.

Created by Balloonatics Designs Inc., Bouquet Builder is the heart of our process
to lead our lovely balloon industry into the next generation.

If you haven't tried Builder before, follow our Beginner tutorial to get started!

Where can I place orders from?

Bouquet Builder can be used from anywhere, but we currently fulfill delivery orders within Metro Vancouver (call for availability), or orders for pick-up in our store.

When do I pay for my bouquet?

After you make your bouquet, your order is sent to our team along with a copy sent to your
email. Your order has instructions and a unique ID to help pay via phone with a credit card.

If you wish to pick-up at your requested time, please pay ahead of time so we may confirm
your order details. If you choose to pay in store, your order is added into our active queue and
produced as soon as possible.

Start a Bouquet!

Welcome! Bouquet Builder is a tool that lets you build your perfect balloon bouquet. It's free to use, and you can order the bouquet for pick-up or delivery* in just a few steps!

The Beginner User is guided with arrows like these (click to dismiss) and short messages.

The Builder works in 3 easy steps:

STEP 1: Choose a Template!

Pick the structure you want your bouquet to have. There's quite a few styles, and always more being added!



STEP 2: Choose your Balloons!

Some Templates have more balloon styles than others, especially the top balloon!



STEP 3: Send your Bouquet!

Our system sends all your build info to our team! Once payment is confirmed, we'll build your bouquet for your specified date!
